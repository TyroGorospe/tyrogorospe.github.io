Music Genres!

Music genres feel like a library, or a grocery store. There are aisles of items that keep your favorite things to read or buy.

Some of these genres grow so much on you that you have to avoid them at some point. One of those genres I was addicted to was J-Pop.

Until now, J-Pop is still a common genre to see in my liked songs and playlists. I can't avoid it. It's just that I listened to it 24/7 when it was around 2018-2019.

The pandemic didn't just help me grow, but my music taste and hobbies as well. I was able to expand my likes and dislikes. Find out what sounds good or not. I just needed to have a passion to look into it.
