Who Am I?

I am Kristan Tyro T. Gorospe. I'm 15 years old and I study in PSHS-CLC. My hobbies consist of playing video games, playing the piano, and listening to music.

I normally play games such as League of Legends, Genshin Impact and Monster Hunter(I don't like sharing about the first two). If it seems fun, I'll play it.

Playing the piano is also one of the few hobbies I'm passionate about. I like to practice songs from game OSTs. My skill may not be the best, but being able to play the songs I love properly is such a rewarding feeling.

Because this website is about music, I can listen to anything (except Country). I like soundtracks from popular games, indie songs that nobody ever heard of, or maybe a few popular songs as well. I feel like my taste in music is flexible but it does has its limits.
