The Place of Playlists

Hi, I'm Tyro. I'm here to talk about music. Music is great. I love music! From Pop to Rap to Indie, as long as it sounds good, I'm into it.

Personally, I don't think I am familiar with mainstream music. Sure, they could sound good, but I feel like I'm most comfortable with songs that aren't well known.

Since this is my website, I get to talk about music that I like. So say goodbye to the songs you hear on the radio every single day. (However, I do want to talk about mainstream music as well. This webpage is about music, after all.)
