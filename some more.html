Rap

Rap is a strange genre, I used to not like it because of how some rap songs would just be about fame, money, and gangs but after taking a look at rappers who actually try to give meaning to their songs then I was able to get into rap.

I was surprised to find out that rap songs can really have messages and meaning to their lyrics. You just need to take a good look at the lyrics and understand the writing.

Just because I like rap now doesn't mean I like everything in the genre. There's just a huge portion of rap that I really cannot get myself into.
